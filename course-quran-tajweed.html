Qur'an with Tajweed

Learn to recite the Qur'an with proper Tajweed rules, pronunciation, and understanding of the sacred text

Course Overview

Our Qur'an with Tajweed course teaches students to recite the Holy Qur'an correctly according to the rules of Tajweed. Tajweed is the science of reciting the Qur'an as it was revealed, with proper pronunciation, articulation, and rhythm.

This course is taught with the utmost respect and reverence for the sacred text. Students learn not only the technical rules of Tajweed but also develop a deeper connection with the Qur'an through understanding and proper recitation.

Who This Course Is For

  • Muslims of all ages wanting to learn proper Qur'anic recitation
  • Children beginning their Qur'anic education
  • Adults seeking to improve their recitation
  • Those who can read Arabic but want to learn Tajweed
  • Students preparing for Qur'an memorization (Hifz)
  • Anyone wanting to recite the Qur'an with proper pronunciation

Learning Outcomes

By the end of this course, students will be able to:

  • Recite the Qur'an with proper Tajweed rules
  • Understand and apply rules of pronunciation (Makharij)
  • Master the characteristics of Arabic letters (Sifaat)
  • Apply rules of elongation (Madd)
  • Understand rules of stopping and starting (Waqf and Ibtida)
  • Recite with proper rhythm and melody (Tarteel)
  • Identify and correct common recitation mistakes
  • Develop confidence in Qur'anic recitation

Curriculum Outline

Foundation Level

  • Introduction to Tajweed and its importance
  • Arabic alphabet and pronunciation basics
  • Makharij (Points of articulation)
  • Basic Sifaat (Characteristics of letters)
  • Reading simple verses with correct pronunciation

Intermediate Level

  • Advanced Sifaat rules
  • Rules of Noon and Meem (Idgham, Ikhfa, Izhar, Iqlab)
  • Rules of Meem Saakinah
  • Rules of Laam and Raa
  • Madd rules (Natural, Obligatory, Permissible)
  • Reciting longer passages

Advanced Level

  • Complex Tajweed rules
  • Rules of stopping and starting (Waqf and Ibtida)
  • Tarteel (Proper rhythm and pace)
  • Advanced pronunciation techniques
  • Recitation of complete Surahs
  • Preparing for Hifz (Memorization)

Teaching Approach

Our Qur'an with Tajweed course is taught with reverence and precision:

  • Respectful Instruction: Teaching with utmost respect for the Holy Qur'an
  • One-on-One Focus: Individual attention for proper pronunciation
  • Repetition and Practice: Regular practice to master rules
  • Audio Learning: Listening to correct recitation
  • Gradual Progression: Building skills systematically
  • Correction and Feedback: Immediate feedback on recitation
  • Memorization Support: Integration with Hifz for interested students

Session Format

  • Duration: Sessions typically last 30-60 minutes
  • Frequency: Flexible scheduling (2-5 sessions per week recommended)
  • Format: Live online one-on-one or small group sessions
  • Class Size: Prefer one-on-one or very small groups (2-3 students) for optimal learning
  • Materials: Qur'an text, Tajweed books, audio recordings
  • Practice: Daily practice recommended between sessions

Course-Specific FAQ

Do I need to know Arabic to take this course?

Basic knowledge of reading Arabic script is helpful but not always required. We can teach Arabic reading alongside Tajweed for complete beginners.

Can children take this course?

Yes! We welcome children of all ages. Our teaching methods are adapted to be age-appropriate, and we work with parents to ensure the best learning experience for young students.

Will this help me memorize the Qur'an?

Yes, learning Tajweed is essential for proper memorization (Hifz). We can integrate memorization into the course for students interested in Hifz.

How long does it take to master Tajweed?

The time varies depending on the student's starting level, practice frequency, and dedication. Regular practice is key to mastering Tajweed rules.
