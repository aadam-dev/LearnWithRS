Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Learn With RS

What is Learn With RS?

Learn With RS is a purpose-driven online learning platform offering personalized education in English Language, Arabic Language, Psychology, Islamic Studies, and Qur'an with Tajweed. We provide high-quality, flexible online courses for students of all ages.

What is the class format?

All our classes are conducted live online via video conferencing platforms like Zoom or Google Meet. Sessions are interactive, allowing for real-time communication between students and instructors. You can participate from anywhere in the world as long as you have a stable internet connection.

What age groups do you teach?

We welcome students of all ages, from young children (5+) to adults. Our courses are tailored to different age groups and learning levels, ensuring that each student receives age-appropriate instruction and support.

How much do courses cost?

Pricing varies by course and program duration. We offer competitive rates and flexible payment options. For detailed pricing information, please contact us directly. We're happy to discuss payment plans and any available discounts or scholarships.

What do I need to participate in online classes?

To participate in our online classes, you'll need:

  • A computer, tablet, or smartphone with internet access
  • Access to Zoom or the video conferencing platform we use
  • A quiet space for learning
  • Basic materials (notebook, pen) as recommended by your instructor
How do I register for a course?

You can register for courses in several ways:

  • Fill out our online registration form on the Register page
  • Contact us directly via email or WhatsApp
  • Use the contact form to express interest

After registration, we'll contact you within 24-48 hours to confirm your enrollment and discuss scheduling.

Can I try a class before committing?

Yes! We offer trial sessions for new students. This allows you to experience our teaching style and ensure the course is a good fit before making a full commitment. Contact us to learn more about our trial session options.

What time zones do you accommodate?

We work with students from around the world and offer flexible scheduling to accommodate different time zones. When you register, please let us know your timezone and preferred class times, and we'll work to find a schedule that works for you.
