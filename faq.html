Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Learn With RS

Who can enroll in Learn With RS courses?

Learn With RS welcomes students of all ages! Our courses are designed for children, teenagers, and adults. Whether you're a parent looking for quality education for your child, a student seeking to enhance your skills, or an adult learner pursuing personal development, we have programs suited for you.

What are the age requirements for courses?

Age requirements vary by course:

  • English & Arabic Language: All ages (children, teens, adults)
  • Psychology: Typically for teens (13+) and adults
  • Islamic Studies: All ages
  • Qur'an with Tajweed: All ages

If you're unsure about age appropriateness, please contact us and we'll help you find the right course.

What is the class format?

All our courses are conducted online through live, interactive sessions. You'll join your instructor and classmates in real-time, allowing for immediate feedback, questions, and collaborative learning. Classes are designed to be engaging and interactive, not pre-recorded lectures.

How do I know which course level is right for me?

During the enrollment process, we'll assess your current level and learning goals. Our instructors will help you determine whether you should start at beginner, intermediate, or advanced level. Don't worry if you're not sureâ€”we're here to guide you!

What are the course fees and pricing?

Pricing varies by course and program duration. We offer competitive rates and flexible payment options. For detailed pricing information, please contact us directly. We're happy to discuss payment plans and any available discounts or scholarships.

How are classes scheduled?

We work with you to find a schedule that fits your timezone and availability. Classes are typically scheduled in the morning, afternoon, or evening hours. During registration, you can indicate your preferred times, and we'll coordinate accordingly.

Do you offer trial sessions?

Yes! We offer trial sessions for new students so you can experience our teaching style and see if Learn With RS is the right fit for you. Contact us to learn more about our trial session options.

What technology do I need to participate?

You'll need:

  • A computer, tablet, or smartphone with internet connection
  • A webcam and microphone (for interactive participation)
  • Access to Zoom or the video conferencing platform we use
  • A quiet space for learning

We'll provide all necessary links and instructions before classes begin.

Will I receive a certificate upon completion?

Yes, students who successfully complete their courses will receive a certificate of completion. Certificates can be used for documentation, school submissions, or personal records. Please note that certificate requirements may vary by course.

Can I enroll in multiple courses at once?

Absolutely! Many students enroll in multiple courses simultaneously. We'll work with you to create a schedule that allows you to balance your learning across different subjects without feeling overwhelmed.

What if I miss a class?

We understand that life happens! If you need to miss a class, please notify us in advance when possible. We'll work with you to provide make-up options or catch-up materials. Regular attendance is encouraged for the best learning experience.

How do I register for a course?

Registration is simple! You can:

  1. Fill out our online registration form on the Register page
  2. Contact us directly via email or WhatsApp
  3. Use the contact form to express interest

After you register, we'll contact you within 24-48 hours to confirm your enrollment and discuss next steps.

Do you offer group classes or one-on-one sessions?

We offer both group classes and one-on-one sessions, depending on the course and your preferences. Group classes provide collaborative learning opportunities, while one-on-one sessions offer more personalized attention. Let us know your preference during registration.

How do I contact support if I have issues?

We're here to help! You can reach us via:

  • Email: info@learnwithrs.com
  • WhatsApp: [Your WhatsApp Number]
  • Contact form on our website

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days.

Is Learn With RS suitable for homeschooling families?

Yes! Learn With RS is an excellent complement to homeschooling. Our courses provide structured learning, expert instruction, and can be used to fulfill educational requirements. Many homeschooling families use our programs as part of their curriculum.

Still Have Questions?

We're here to help! Contact us and we'll get back to you as soon as possible.
